<b>Anonimus Chat Rules</b>

<b>1. Age 18+</b>
The service is available only to users aged 18 or older. By registering, you confirm that you meet this requirement.

<b>2. Anonymity</b>
Do not share personal data: phone numbers, addresses, social media links, payment details, or any information that could identify you or your partner. The bot hides Telegram contacts, but it cannot protect data you choose to send yourself.

<b>3. P2P chats (live partner)</b>
• Be respectful — no insults or threats.
• Illegal material, spam, advertising, and harassment are prohibited.
• Contacts, location, and forwarded messages are blocked in P2P chats.
• If rules are broken, use Report or Block — moderators receive a signal, and blocked pairs will not be matched again for 24 hours.

<b>4. AI dialogs</b>
AI chats are for entertainment. Personas generate replies automatically; this is not professional advice and not a real person.

<b>5. Adult content and Premium</b>
Some content may be marked as adult and shown blurred. Premium removes blur on adult content in AI dialogs. Premium does not change behavior rules and does not grant priority in regular P2P (M+M, F+M).

<b>6. Telegram Stars payments</b>
Premium and individual purchases are paid via Telegram Stars. Prices are shown before payment. Refunds are available only where Telegram rules allow and in case of a technical error on our side — contact support with your payment ID.

<b>7. Account deletion</b>
You can delete your profile from the Profile menu. After deletion, personal data is marked as removed; backups and aggregated statistics may be retained for up to 30 days per service policy.

<b>8. Disclaimer</b>
The bot does not guarantee that AI mode connects you to a real person. In P2P mode your partner is a live user, but the service does not verify their identity. Use the service at your own risk.
